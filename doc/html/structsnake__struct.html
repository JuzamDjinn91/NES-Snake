§ body_element_coordinates

unsigned char body_element_coordinates[SNAKE_MAX_SIZE<< 1]

Array of snakes body-coordinates (pixelbased), two elements are a coordinate set, eg. [0] is the x-coordinate of the first body-element and [1] its y-coordinate.

unsigned char size_index
